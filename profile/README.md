- **Scene Management** – Save camera angles, styles, and section cuts for animations.

- **3D Warehouse Integration** – Download millions of free models without leaving **Software SketchUp Pro**.
- **VR/AR Export** – Send models to VR headsets or AR mobile apps directly from **Software SketchUp Pro**.
- **Collaboration Tools** – Trimble Connect cloud sync for team-based design reviews.
- **Energy Simulation** – Link **Software SketchUp Pro** to OpenStudio and EnergyPlus for green building analysis.
